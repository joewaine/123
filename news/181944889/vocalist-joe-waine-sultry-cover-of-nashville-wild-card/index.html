Vocalist Joe Waine Delivers Sultry Cover Of Nashville's "Wild Card"

Strange Beautiful Cover From the Hit Show Nashville for a Seattle Compilation
by Alyssa Dixon Mar 30, 2020

Joe Waine was asked to provide a cover for a compilation dedicated to the raunchy TV hit show Nashville. He chose 'Wild Card' from Season 4. He Transformed the track into a psychedelic bossa nova ballroom number, lush with vibraphone, saxaphone help from a talented Russian musician named Oleg that he met on the internet. Enjoy the swirls of spanish guitar and the swells of strings and tickles of the harp. Guitar accompaniment by Aron Pollard of Pleasures. Mixed and mastered by River Nason.
